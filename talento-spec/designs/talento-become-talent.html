The Talento Identity System
Your Key. Your Control.

One identity. Infinite protection.

When you register, Talento issues you a Talento Identity Key (TIK) — a unique cryptographic credential that belongs to you permanently. No platform, studio, or buyer can revoke it.

Every single time your likeness is used in a production, a unique project key is generated from your TIK. That project key identifies the buyer, the production, the use type, and the territory. You can see every single one in your dashboard.

If a buyer exceeds their license — uses your face in a country they didn't pay for, in content you didn't approve, beyond the time they licensed — the system flags it automatically. You always know who has your face. And so do we.

Anatomy of Your Key
TL · AX93F·K28Q·771 · PRJ042 · STU009
TL — Talento platform identifier
AX93F·K28Q·771 — Your unique personal identity key. Permanent. Yours forever.
PRJ042 — Unique project code. Generated per production. Tracks exact use.
STU009 — Buyer/studio identifier. Who used your likeness, and when.

Your Rules
You Decide What You Allow

This is not a checkbox you tick and forget. Your permissions are live, enforceable rules built into every license key that includes your identity.

Change your mind? Update your permissions and every future license reflects it instantly. Existing licenses run to their agreed term — your commitments are honored — but nothing new gets issued outside your current rules.

Want to allow gaming but not advertising? Fine. UK and EU only? Done. No adult content, ever? Enforced at the API level — no studio can override it.

This is the difference between a contract no-one reads and a system that actually works at machine speed.
