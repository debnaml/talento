03 — Auction
Make It a Moment.
Auction a Role.

The most powerful marketing tool in AI film isn't a trailer. It's a casting competition. Auction a named role in your production — fight the lead actor in a Hong Kong back alley, die alongside a war hero, steal a scene from your star — and let the public bid to be in it.

The winner gets their face in the film. The production gets press it couldn't buy. Everyone wins.

Auction roles are listed on the Talento Auctions page, featured in the live ticker across the entire platform, and promoted to our 48,000+ talent community — many of whom are fans, enthusiasts, and industry insiders who share, talk, and amplify.

Every bid is a qualified, consented, licensed participant. The winner's Talento Identity Key is verified. Their project key is generated. The license is clean. The performance is yours.

🎯
Define the Role
Name the scene. Describe what the winner gets — fight your lead actor, appear in the crowd of history's greatest battle, stand face-to-face with your villain. The more specific, the more excited the bids.
⏱
Set the Timer
Open the auction for 24, 48, or 72 hours. Set a reserve price or open it free. The countdown drives urgency. Bidding activity is shown live across the Talento platform.
📣
Platform-Wide Promotion
Your auction appears in the live ticker on every Talento page, in the Auctions hub, and in email alerts to talent matching your brief. 48,000+ people see it.
🔑
Winner is Keyed. License is Clean.
The winning bidder's Talento Identity Key is verified. A unique project key is generated. Consent is confirmed. The license is issued. No paperwork. No lawyers. Done.
🏅
Runner-Up Packages
Don't waste the interest. Top 10 bidders can be offered background packages — appear in the same scene, one step removed. Revenue for the production. Satisfaction for the bidders.
